Gate Management

It's time for societies in India to reinvent gate management. Chowkidar removes the burden of calling residents whenever a visitor arrives. It digitalizes and strengthens gate management stopping trespassers and enabling the gatekeepers to manage security operations, paperless.

Daily Staff Management

Connect your residents to cooks, maids, and caretakers; implement a rating system for daily staff, efficiently manage the attendance, time of entry and exit.

Hall Booking

Streamline your hall booking process with Chowkidar. The app can be used to avoid duplicate booking, time slot management, billing and even sending out invites to all the guests through SMS notification. It brings about new efficiencies in hall booking and mitigates human errors.
